Kabu Hydro Electric Project

Burundi, South Africa

For the Kabu Hydro Electric Project on the Kaburantwa River, we worked on the lighting calculation of indoor, outdoor, switchyard, transformer yard as well as exterior roads. We did the selection of lighting fixtures to match the design criteria documents. We worked on the circuit load distribution, cable sizing, db sizing and the Single line Diagram (SLD) and layout preparations.

We also did the preparation of lighting schedule, cable schedule and billing of materials etc. We did complete coordination with the vendor of the lighting products and other materials.

  • Client: Kabu Hydroelectric Power Plant
  • Category: Industrial Lighting

NER Power Management Project

Power Grid Coroporation of India (PGCIL)

We woked on the complete illumination of 66/33kV substation viz, indoor, outdoor switchyard, street lighting, DIALux based lux calculation, Single Line Diagram (SLD) and layout prepartaions, billing of material and report, as well as vendor support and site coordination.

  • Client: Power Grid Coroporation of India (PGCIL)
  • Category: Industrial Lighting

Galaxy Apartment

Okhla, New Delhi

Our team calculated the lighting lux level of the lobby, drawing room and various other rooms, as per the standards and to avoid strain on the eyes. We also worked on the lighting layout, wiring diagram, bill of materials, cable schedule etc.
